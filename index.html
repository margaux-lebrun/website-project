Our goal

Data conseil is a branch of the association data minds, whose aim is to spread awerness about data sciences on HEC Paris' campus. But we are a bit more special: we recruit people with particular knowledge on data, code and councelling, and we then work with firms to find adapted solutions to their problematics with data.

Why you should choose us

All member of the team are experienced students. They can therefore bring you new visions and new angles. The diversity of the team allows us to find the exact right profiles to best adress your issues. And above all, as students, we offer extrem flexibility and a strong expertise way more easily than a secialized agency would.
